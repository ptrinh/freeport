A Freeport mini-app demo — not a real insurer
This is a Freeport mini-app. Open it inside Freeport (Settings → Experimental → Mini-apps, add freeport.network/insurance-store) to get a quote against your Freeport identity, reputation & wallet.
Pick a policy. We underwrite the premium from your Freeport profile — your reputation and account history (public, read from your npub) plus your wallet balance and region (private, shared only with your approval). Demo only: the form auto-fills, and payment goes to a donation address.
