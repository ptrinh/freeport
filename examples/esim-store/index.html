eSIM Demo Shop

A Freeport mini-app demo — nothing here is a real eSIM
This shop is a Freeport mini-app. Open the Freeport app → Settings → Experimental → Mini-apps, then add apps.freeport.network/esim-store to shop with your Freeport identity & wallet.
Demo of the mini-app bridge: sign in with your Nostr key (NIP-07) and pay with the built-in wallet — the shop never sees your private key, and every payment needs your approval in a native Freeport dialog.
