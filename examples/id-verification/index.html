This is a Freeport mini-app. Open it inside Freeport (Settings → Experimental → Mini-apps, add apps.freeport.network/id-verification) to verify your identity against your npub.
Human-verified identity for your Freeport profile. Submit your document, book a short video call, pay in USDT — after the call an agent issues a public certificate link tied to your npub that you can put on your profile. Demo only: the form auto-fills, no call happens, and payment goes to a donation address.
