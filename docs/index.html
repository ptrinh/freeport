The marketplace with no middleman.

Freeport is a decentralized, peer-to-peer marketplace for rides, services and goods — built on Nostr. No company in the middle, no server to run, no commission, no account. Your keys, your price.

Why Freeport

A real peer-to-peer network — not another platform taking a cut and holding your data.

🤝

No middleman

Deal directly with the other person and agree your own price. No platform fee, no surge pricing.

🔑

No sign-up

Your identity is a secure key created on your device — no email, no phone-number account, no password.

💸

Your price

Negotiate openly with a built-in estimator. Zero commission means more stays with you.

🔒

Private by design

Messages are end-to-end encrypted. Listings carry only a coarse, neighborhood-level location — never your exact spot.

🌐

No backend needed

The app has no server of its own — it talks straight to open, public Nostr relays. Nothing for you to host, and no single company can shut it down, censor it, or sell your data.

⭐

Earn reputation

A transparent, peer-rated karma system builds trust between strangers, with no central authority.

How it works

Post or browse, negotiate, done — all between you and the other party.

Post or browse

Request a ride or service, or browse what people nearby are offering.

Negotiate

Offers and counter-offers go back and forth privately until you both agree.

Make the deal

Confirm, swap contact details over an encrypted message, and meet up.

What's inside

A full marketplace toolkit — self-custodial, encrypted, and yours. The newest pieces are opt-in: flip them on under Settings → Experimental.

⚡

Built-in Lightning wallet

Self-custodial Bitcoin Lightning + USDT wallet, derived from your Nostr key — one backup covers identity and funds. Your own lightning address, QR pay, and one-tap Pay on confirmed deals.

💬

Encrypted chat & calls

1:1 encrypted chat with voice messages, reactions, disappearing messages and in-chat payments — plus peer-to-peer audio/video calls with no call server in the middle.

🛡️

Escrow without a custodian

Conditional Lightning payments (HODL invoices) on confirmed deals. The protocol enforces the lock — nobody ever holds the money in between, and unreleased funds auto-refund.

🧩

Mini-apps

Third-party web apps run inside Freeport with your portable identity and wallet — sandboxed behind a permission firewall where every sensitive action needs your approval. Add any app by URL or QR.

🏪

Shops & zaps

Durable seller storefronts with a conversational checkout, and Lightning zap tipping on posts with verifiable receipts.

🤖

On-device AI

Describe what you need in any language and it drafts your post; incoming messages auto-translate. Runs entirely on your device — your words never leave it.

📣

Telegram bridge

Market feeds in your Telegram groups, one-tap broadcast of group posts to the open network, and personal pings when something needs you.

🌍

Runs everywhere, speaks everything

iOS, Android, web, and desktop apps — plus the whole app as a single offline HTML file. 56 languages with full right-to-left support.

🔓

Open source, made to fork

MIT-licensed, no investor deck. Any community can stand up its own market — own name, city, relays — by changing configuration, not architecture.

Why Freeport exists

🔬

It's a research project

How far can pure P2P infrastructure go? Buyers, sellers, riders, drivers meet over Nostr, settle in Lightning or cash. Nothing in between needs permission to exist.

📈

It's a thesis

A P2P marketplace with a reputation system works. When people own their reputation, the market self-regulates.

⚖️

It's a check and balance

A zero-fee exit that answers to no one puts a ceiling on how greedy the big platforms can get.

🎈

It's fun

An open protocol, Claude Code as a pair programmer, no investor deck.

🚫

Nobody profits — including me

No operator, no cut. A protocol, not a platform. Run it or fork it.

⚠️

No one will protect you here

That's the point, and the price. No support line, no refunds, no arbiter. Reputation and your own judgment are the whole safety net.
